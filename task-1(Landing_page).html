About Us

RNK Travels is a travel agency based in Jaigaon, India. Established in 2018, they specialize in providing travel packages and tours to various destinations in Northeast India, with a focus on the majestic kingdom of Bhutan.

  • Exclusive tour packages tailored to client preferences.
  • Dedicated and experienced team to ensure a memorable experience.

If you're looking for an unforgettable adventure in Northeast India or Bhutan, RNK Travels could be the perfect travel partner for you!
